# Privacy Policy for VoidRoll

### Last updated: 20-11-2025

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

### Interpretation and Definitions

- App: Refers to VoidRoll, the software program provided by the Company.
- You: The individual accessing or using the Service.
- Device: Means any device that can access the Service such as a computer, a cellphone, or a digital tablet.

### Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

VoidRoll does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) on external servers.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not track your location, usage patterns, or personal details.

### Local Storage

The App uses your device's internal storage (e.g., Shared Preferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS) to save your preferences, including:

- Dice presets
- Roll history
- App theme settings
- Character sheet data

This data resides entirely on your personal device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App or clear the App's data, this information will be deleted from your device.

### Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

### Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us. If You click on a third-party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit.

### Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
